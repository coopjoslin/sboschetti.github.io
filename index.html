About me

Hi! I'm Sianna Boschetti. I'm a Content and Production Specialist for the National Apartment Association. I was an intern with the Urban Land Institute Foundation in 2020, and an inaugural member of the EMILY's List Ignite Change Fellowship. I'm currently earning my professional certificate in full-stack development from MIT's xPro program. Reach out and let's connect!
